How to choose the right size

The maker chart uses garment measurements in inches. Compare it with a shirt or pant that already fits well.

Adult shirt

Choose standard shirt size mainly from Full chest and Length. Shoulder is a cross-check.

Adult pant / shorts

Choose standard pant size mainly from Length and Relaxed waist. Half hip is a cross-check.

Kids

Measure a well-fitting item and enter the exact inches. The maker can use those measurements directly.
